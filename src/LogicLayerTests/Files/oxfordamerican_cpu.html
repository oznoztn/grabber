    'Adverse' or 'Averse'?

    Which of the following is correct?

    • You can leave if the weather's adverse
    • You can leave if the weather's averse

    Which of the following is correct?

    • I'm adverse to the idea of marriage
    • I'm averse to the idea of marriage

    Which of the following is correct?

    • There was no adverse impact on service
    • There was no averse impact on service

    Which of the following is correct?

    • Even small businesses are debt adverse
    • Even small businesses are debt averse

    Which of the following is correct?

    • It was a step to which I was very averse
    • It was a step to which I was very adverse

    Which of the following is correct?

    • She had an adverse reaction to nuts
    • She had an averse reaction to nuts

    Which of the following is correct?

    • They have to take a risk averse view
    • They have to take a risk adverse view

    • Is the effect averse or beneficial?

    Which of the following is correct?

    • Check your car in adverse weather
